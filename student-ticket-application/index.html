Application for a student ticket

If you are a student and would like to attend RailsCamp Sydney, but find the cost of a full price ticket prohibitive, then you are ellegible to apply for a $50 student ticket.

Student tickets are paid for by our amazing sponsors and are limited in number. Please complete the application form below.

Applicants will be notified via email in due course about the success of their application. Successful applicants will be invited to purchase a $50 ticket.
